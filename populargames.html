Popular Games


                 
                 

1. God of War

It's a adventure and action game created by David Jaffe's. It is based on a guy called Kratos who is sent by the Greek gods to kill Ares.

2. GTA V

It's a action based game created by the Rockstar Games. It's one of the best games of all time. The story mode game is set in the city of Los Santos. It is a real life simulation kind of game.

3. Minecraft

Minecraft is a survival game created by Mojang studios. It's like a virtual world where players can start their own civilisation, create castles and use the natural resources efficiently.

4. Fortnite

Fortnite is a battle royale game. Players in this game compete with other players in order to win. The last man standing wins the game.

5. Zelda

This game is actually about a boy whose name is Link. The boy gets involved in a mysterious and magical quest to save the princess of Hyrule kingdom.

6. Red Dead Redemption II

This game is set in the 19th century. It spotlights the life of Arthur Morgan and his occupation in the Van der Linde gang.

7. Spider-man Miles Morales

This game is about a teenager named Miles Morale who is adapting to his new position as spider-man.

8. Forza Horizon 5

Forza Horizon 5 is a Xbox exclusive game.It is a free world racing game which flows in fast pace.
